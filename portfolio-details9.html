H1B Employer and Sales Dashboard

H1B Employer and Trend Analysis Dashboard

Stakeholder Requirements: Stakeholders needed approval trends for H1B petitions by employer, city, state, and year. They required insights on approval vs. denial rates to make informed decisions and a geographical breakdown for petition filings.

Dashboard Components:

Filters (State, City, Employers, Year): Filters allow dynamic data views based on state, city, and year. This enables stakeholders to focus on specific data subsets.
KPI Cards: Summarizes initial approvals, continuing approvals, denials, etc. for quick insights.
Trend Analysis Chart: Year-over-year line chart for approvals and denials.
Map: A geographical visualization of filings and approvals across the U.S.
Employers Table: Displays detailed metrics for key employers' approvals and denials.

Insights: The map highlights H1B hotspots, while the trend chart shows seasonal changes. The KPIs summarize 516K initial approvals and 1M continuing approvals. The table identifies top employers (e.g., Infosys Limited).

Possible Improvements: Adding denial reasons, approval rate percentages, and drill-through pages for deeper analysis.

Superstore Sales Dashboard

Stakeholder Requirements: The stakeholders wanted insights into overall sales, profitability, and returns across regions and customer segments.

Dashboard Components:

Filters (Region Selection): Filters allow users to switch between different U.S. regions.
KPI Cards: Displays total sales, profit, quantities sold, and returns.
Sales by Segment (Pie Chart): Visualizes sales contributions by Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.
Monthly Sales and Profit Trends (Line Charts): Shows monthly sales and profit trends, comparing year-over-year changes.
Sales by Ship Mode (Bar Chart): Provides insights on shipping mode contributions.
Sales by Payment Mode: Pie chart displaying customer preferences for payment modes.
Sales by State (Map): Displays state-wise sales and profit.
Sales by Category and Sub-Category (Bar Charts): Breaks down sales by categories and sub-categories.

Insights: California leads in sales. Standard Class accounts for the highest shipments. Office Supplies is the top category, with Phones, Chairs, and Binders as top sub-categories.

Possible Improvements: Adding a return rate KPI, drill-through to view specific order details, and including forecasting for future sales trends.
